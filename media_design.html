Interior Designing

The interior design market worldwide from 2013 to 2021. In 2016, the global interior design market was valued at approximately 121.1 billion U.S. dollars

Be it a place of residence or a one of work, we believe in designing spaces that give the four walls an identity. We at D.R.S MEDIA & DESIGN SCHOOL, hone the skills of every budding interior designer and equip them with the tools they need to breathe life into the walls.

DIPLOMA IN INTERIOR DESIGNING (2 Years)

The Two Year course is devised to provide you with all the knowledge and information required by a professional interior designer including professional practice, budgeting, and estimating quantities and cost. Along with this, you will also learn to design and handle commercial spaces such as office, retail, and hospitality design. On completion of this course, you will be equipped with everything you need to work as independent interior designers capable of handling all kinds of residential and commercial spaces.

B.SC INTERIOR DESIGNING (3 Years)

This program is to make our students culturally, socially, and environmentally aware, and who have the knowledge and skills to create interior spaces that are efficient, client focused and sustainable. Students who complete this program will be fully equipped to start their interior design career.

Fashion Designing

The global apparel market is projected to grow in value from 1.3 trillion U.S. dollars in 2018 to about 1.5 trillion dollars in 2022, showing that the demand for clothing and shoes is on the rise across the world. The regional distribution of the demand share of apparel is expected to stay mostly consistent in that period, although the Asia Pacific region had the highest level of growth at four percent.

Fashion is Art and Clothes are your Canvas. Fashion Design is a multidimensional profession that involves the seamless blend of one’s design skills and creative imagination. It is an art that involves designing clothes keeping in mind the consumer’s tastes and preferences. At DRS MEDIA & DESIGN SCHOOL, We encourage our students to come up with creations that are fashion-forward and wearable but at the same time giving them the space to visualize and create unconventional and out-of-the-box garments.

B.SC COSTUME & FASHION DESIGNING (3 Years)

Designed to provide you with a comprehensive overview of the fashion industry, this program includes the study of product development, sourcing, buying, marketing, retailing, and merchandising. Learn the core principles of draping by constructing corset dress and jackets etc. This course will also help you develop an individual collection to showcase your unique philosophy and design strengths. The completion of the three-year course will enable me to work as a Designer, Visual Merchandiser, Visual Artist, Production Designer, Pattern Coordinator, and Editor.

DIPLOMA IN FASHION DESIGNING (2 Years)

This curriculum focuses on developing your understanding of key theoretical concepts and on practicing skills associated with the areas of clothing, textiles, and fashion. Practical application of the knowledge and skills will be demonstrated in projects reflected at both the personal and work level.
