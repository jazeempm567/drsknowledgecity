About School

DEMS is a private CBSE affiliated school which established in 1983.The school located in the heart of Changaramkulam, Malappuram Dist. It is one among the first hundred schools of India which got CBSE Affiliation. DEMS is a well reputed school with a very good track record of academics for the last three decades. It has been the center for CBSE Board examinations and NIOS examinations for many years.

Our students who stepped into DEMS to full fill their dreams are now in different part of world with high competency in Medical, Engineering, Education, Finance, IT & Administration sectors. DEMS is proud of the students who achieved all the heights in their life and what they have been learned to be a good human above the education. At Darussalam English School, the administrators, faculties and management work as a team to create an enthusiastic and welcoming environment where students can become lifelong leaders. We are committed to academic excellence, actively work to encourage personal development of our students. We emphasize the importance of physical and mental well-being, and we believe that as a result, our students become the best version of themselves.

Our Mission

Darussalam English School provides an engaging and challenging learning environment where students are encouraged to excel intellectually, academically and socially.

We believe that all students have the potential to be active, independent thinkers and learners. Confidence and motivation are the seeds of life-long learning and with these, education can be an exploration of ideas and values. Student engagement is critical to inspire students to pursue their own interests and push their personal limits of achievement. An effective education must prepare students with the skills needed for success in an ever-changing world.

Our Vision

We nurture our students’ natural desires to learn with a personalized educational programmed, project-based learning and guided enquiry. These promote the development of independent thinking, critical analysis, emotional intelligence and essential communication skills.

Our academic program is designed to be stimulating and challenging. Students respond positively to the encouragement of our academic. Life at DEMS is lively, full and fun. We put the personal experience of each child at the heart of everything we do.

Our Values

Our culture is based on a set of four values - Trust, Care, Innovation, Pride This shape our Facuities and students’ behaviors and hence the way the DEMS behaves. It ensures we are all taking care the students from a commonly understood base that can be consistently applied

Our values need to be lived every day, used to help faculties, students and parents through any challenges we may face in the Education, Character, Health & Safety of Students and Faculties which help us recognize and celebrate our achievements

It is important that we hold ourselves and others accountable for our values every day and have defined a set of behaviors that are expected from all of us. They describe how our behaviors bring DEMS values to life.

Trust

We work hard to earn trust and respect from our students and parents We deliver on our promises; straight forward and honest; do the right thing; and take personal responsibility for getting things done.

Care

We care deeply about our student’s and faculties. We work together to deliver high-quality of education services. We take care of each other and we aim to make a positive difference to students lives.

Innovation

We aspire to be better than anyone else at what we do. We continue improve our ways of technology and try new ideas, big and small. Faculties and parents share their knowledge and experience and embrace change.

Pride

We want to be proud of what we do. We know the education is important, and we take pride in giving out to students. We value the energy and enthusiasm, skill and experience, and an ability to make hard work fun. We contribute both as individuals and as part of a team.
