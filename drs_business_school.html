BBA AIRLINE & AIRPORT MANAGEMENT

BBA in airline & airport management airline & airport management is a bachelor level program designed to provide training for managerial and administrative roles in the aviation industry. An airport is a busy place so it requires a great number of workers and staff members to operate efficiently and smoothly. Experienced staff is also required to take care of factors such as safety and protection, technological factors, finances, etc. There is a huge requirement of experienced managers and staff to handle all of these activities. Candidates who are interested in managing all the aviation and airport activities may enrol for this 3 years bachelor degree. BBA LOGISTICS & SUPPLY CHAIN MANAGEMENT

BBA in Logistics and Supply Chain Management is a three-year bachelor degree available in the domain of Logistics and Supply Chain. Logistics is basically related to the commercial activities of transporting goods to the customer. Logistics management involves the management of all activities related to transportation and logistics.
The three years BBA in Logistics and Supply Chain Management trains students for processes and concepts related to logistics. The program also provides knowledge about implementing, controlling and planning the efficient flow of goods from the supplier to the customers.
Some important topics which will be covered in this course are HR Management, Finance, Marketing, Retail Management, International Trade and Logistics. Apart from this, the program also includes a six month training period which allows the students to understand the trade concepts. Students who have some skills such as leadership, communication, team building, technology and managerial are the ideal candidates for BBA in Logistics and Supply Chain Management. They can choose any college for admission to the BBA in Logistics and Supply Chain Management program after checking its fee structure and intake.
